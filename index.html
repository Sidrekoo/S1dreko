Our Story

Built by Gamers,
For Gamers

Founded in 2022, SIDREKO was born from a simple idea: gamers deserve a store that truly understands them. No filler, no fluff — just the best gear, delivered to your door.

The SIDREKO Story

We started as a small group of students at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo with one shared dream: to bring world-class gaming gear to Filipino gamers at fair prices.

Today, we serve hundreds of customers across the Philippines and continue to grow. Every product in our catalog is personally tested by our team before it goes live on the site.

Our community of 50,000+ players is our greatest achievement. When they win, we win.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every Filipino gamer with access to professional-grade gaming equipment at honest, accessible prices — regardless of budget or location.

🌐

Our Vision

A Philippines where every gamer has the tools to compete at the highest level, locally and internationally, supported by a thriving gaming community.

⚡

Innovation First

We work alongside top hardware engineers and pro players to identify emerging technology before it goes mainstream. Find tomorrow's gear here, today.

🤝

Community Driven

Our product recommendations and curation are powered by real feedback from real players. No sponsored rankings, no paid placements — only honest picks.
